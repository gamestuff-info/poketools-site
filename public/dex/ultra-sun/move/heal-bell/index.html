Moves - Heal Bell

Categories: Unique

The user makes a soothing bell chime to heal the status conditions of all the party Pokémon.

    In triple battles, this move can be used on either side to target the farthest away opposing Pokémon.

Removes major status effects and confusion from every Pokémon in the user's party.
