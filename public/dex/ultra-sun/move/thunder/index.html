Moves - Thunder

Categories: Ailment, Damage

A wicked thunderbolt is dropped on the target to inflict damage. This may also leave the target with paralysis.

Description

Inflicts regular damage. Has a 30% chance to paralyze the target.

During Rain Dance, this move has 100% accuracy. It also has a100−accuracy percent chance to break through the protection of Protect and Detect.

During Sunny Day, this move has 50% accuracy.
