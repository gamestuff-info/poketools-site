Moves - Bug Buzz

Categories: Damage, Lower

The user generates a damaging sound wave by vibration. This may also lower the target’s Sp. Def stat.

Classspecial class

Special Effects

  • Special Defense decreased by 1 (10% chance)

Flags

  • Blocked by Detect and Protect

    This move will not work if the target has used Detect or Protect this turn.

  • Copied by Mirror Move

    A Pokémon targeted by this move can use Mirror Move to copy it.

  • Sound-based

    Pokémon with Soundproof are immune to this move.

  • Ignores Substitute

    This move ignores the target's Substitute.

Description

Inflicts regular damage. Has a 10% chance to lower the target's Special Defense by one stage.
