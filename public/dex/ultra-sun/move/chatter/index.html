Moves - Chatter

Categories: Ailment, Damage

The user attacks the target with sound waves of deafening chatter. This confuses the target.

Stats

TypeFlying
Classspecial class
Power65
Accuracy100
PP20
TargetSelected Pokémon
PriorityNormal

Special Effects

  • Inflicts Confusion on the target(s). (100% chance)
  • Lasts 2-5 turns.

Flags

  • Blocked by Detect and Protect

    This move will not work if the target has used Detect or Protect this turn.

  • Copied by Mirror Move

    A Pokémon targeted by this move can use Mirror Move to copy it.

  • Sound-based

    Pokémon with Soundproof are immune to this move.

  • Targets opposite side in triples

    In triple battles, this move can be used on either side to target the farthest away opposing Pokémon.

  • Ignores Substitute

    This move ignores the target's Substitute.

Description

Inflicts regular damage. Has either a 1%, 11%, or 31% chance to confuse the target, based on the volume of the recording made for this move; louder recordings increase the chance of confusion. If the user is not a Chatot, this move will not cause confusion.

This move cannot be copied by Mimic, Mirror Move, or Sketch, nor selected by Assist, Metronome, or Sleep Talk.

Similar moves

These moves have the same effect, but their stats and effect chances may differ.
