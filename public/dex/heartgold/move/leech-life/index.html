Moves - Leech Life

Categories: Heal, Damage

A blood-draining attack. The user’s HP is restored by half the damage taken by the target.

Stats

TypeBug
Classphysical class
Power20
Accuracy100
PP15
TargetSelected Pokémon
PriorityNormal

Special Effects

  • User heals itself by 50% of the damage inflicted.

Flags

  • Makes contact

    User touches the target. This triggers some abilities (e.g., Static) and items (e.g., Sticky Barb).

  • Blocked by Detect and Protect

    This move will not work if the target has used Detect or Protect this turn.

  • Copied by Mirror Move

    A Pokémon targeted by this move can use Mirror Move to copy it.

  • Heals

    This move is blocked by Heal Block.

Description

Inflicts regular damage. Drains half the damage inflicted to heal the user.
