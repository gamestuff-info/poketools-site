Moves - Perish Song

Categories: Ailment

Any Pokémon that hears this song faints in three turns, unless it switches out of battle.

Stats

Special Effects

  • Inflicts Perish Song on the target(s).
  • Lasts 4 turns.

Flags

  • Sound-based

    Pokémon with Soundproof are immune to this move.

  • Targets opposite side in triples

    In triple battles, this move can be used on either side to target the farthest away opposing Pokémon.

  • Ignores Substitute

    This move ignores the target's Substitute.

Description

Every Pokémon is given a counter that starts at 3 and decreases by 1 at the end of every turn, including this one. When a Pokémon's counter reaches zero, that Pokémon faints. A Pokémon that leaves the field will lose its counter; its replacement does not inherit the effect, and other Pokémon's counters remain.

This effect is passed on by Baton Pass.

This move cannot be copied by Mirror Move.
