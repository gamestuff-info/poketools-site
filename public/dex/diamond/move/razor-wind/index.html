Moves - Razor Wind

Categories: Damage

A two-turn attack. Blades of wind hit the foe on the second turn. It has a high critical-hit ratio.

Flags

    This move has a charging turn that can be skipped with a Power Herb.

    This move will not work if the target has used Detect or Protect this turn.

  • Copied by Mirror Move

    A Pokémon targeted by this move can use Mirror Move to copy it.

Description

Inflicts regular damage. User's critical hit rate is one level higher when using this move. User charges for one turn before attacking.

This move cannot be selected by Sleep Talk.
