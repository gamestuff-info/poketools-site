Quick Feet

Boosts Speed if there is a status problem.

Description

Whenever this Pokémon has a major status ailment, it has 1.5× its Speed. This Pokémon is not affected by the usual Speed cut from paralysis.

Overworld: If the lead Pokémon has this ability, the wild encounter rate is halved.
