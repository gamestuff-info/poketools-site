Moves - Flame Burst

Categories: Damage

The user attacks the target with a bursting flame. The bursting flame damages Pokémon next to the target as well.

Stats

TypeFire
Classspecial class

Accuracy100
PP15
TargetSelected Pokémon

Special Effects

This move has no special effects.

Flags

  • Blocked by Detect and Protect

    This move will not work if the target has used Detect or Protect this turn.

  • Copied by Mirror Move

    A Pokémon targeted by this move can use Mirror Move to copy it.

Description

Inflicts regular damage. If this move successfully hits the target, any Pokémon adjacent to the target are damaged for 1/16 their max HP.
