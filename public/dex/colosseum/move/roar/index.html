Moves - ROAR

Categories: Force Switch

Causes the target to flee and switch out of battle.

Stats

TypeNormal
Classphysical class
Power–
Accuracy100
PP20
TargetSelected Pokémon
Priority-6

Special Effects

This move has no special effects.

Flags

  • Blocked by Detect and Protect

    This move will not work if the target has used DETECT or PROTECT this turn.

  • Copied by Mirror Move

    A Pokémon targeted by this move can use MIRROR MOVE to copy it.

Description

Switches the target out for another of its trainer's Pokémon selected at random. Wild battles end immediately.

Doesn't affect Pokémon with SUCTION CUPS or under the effect of INGRAIN.
