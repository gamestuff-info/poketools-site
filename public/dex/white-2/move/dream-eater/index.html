Moves - Dream Eater

Categories: Heal, Damage

The user eats the dreams of a sleeping target. It absorbs half the damage caused to heal the user’s HP.

Description

Fails if not used on a sleeping Pokémon. Inflicts regular damage. Drains half the damage inflicted to heal the user.
