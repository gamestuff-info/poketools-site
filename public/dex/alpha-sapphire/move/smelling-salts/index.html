Moves - Smelling Salts

Categories: Damage

This attack inflicts double damage on a target with paralysis. This also cures the target’s paralysis, however.

    This move will not work if the target has used Detect or Protect this turn.

  • Copied by Mirror Move

    A Pokémon targeted by this move can use Mirror Move to copy it.

Description

Inflicts regular damage. If the target is paralyzed, this move has double power, and the target is cured of its paralysis.
