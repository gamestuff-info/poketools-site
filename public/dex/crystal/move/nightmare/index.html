Moves - NIGHTMARE

Categories: Ailment

A sleeper loses 1/4 HP every turn.

Stats

TypeGhost
Classphysical class
Power–
Accuracy100
PP15
TargetSelected Pokémon
PriorityNormal

Special Effects

  • Inflicts Nightmare on the target(s).

Flags

  • Blocked by Detect and Protect

    This move will not work if the target has used DETECT or PROTECT this turn.

  • Copied by Mirror Move

    A Pokémon targeted by this move can use MIRROR MOVE to copy it.

Description

Only works on sleeping Pokémon. Gives the target a nightmare, damaging it for 1/4 its max HP every turn. If the target wakes up or leaves the field, this effect ends.
