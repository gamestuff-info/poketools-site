Flags

  • Has a charging turn

    This move has a charging turn that can be skipped with a NO LINK TEXT.

  • Blocked by Detect and Protect

    This move will not work if the target has used DETECT or PROTECT this turn.

  • Copied by Mirror Move

    A Pokémon targeted by this move can use MIRROR MOVE to copy it.

Description

Inflicts regular damage. User charges for one turn before attacking.

During SUNNY DAY, the charge turn is skipped.

During RAIN DANCE or SANDSTORM, power is halved.

This move cannot be selected by SLEEP TALK.
