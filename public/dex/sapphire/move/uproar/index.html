Moves - UPROAR

Categories: Damage

Causes an uproar for 2 to 5 turns and prevents sleep.

Special Effects

This move has no special effects.

Flags

  • Blocked by Detect and Protect

    This move will not work if the target has used DETECT or PROTECT this turn.

  • Copied by Mirror Move

    A Pokémon targeted by this move can use MIRROR MOVE to copy it.

  • Affected by King's Rock

    Pokémon holding a KING'S ROCK have a 10% chance of causing the target to flinch.

Description

Inflicts regular damage. User is forced to use this move for 2–5 turns, selected at random. All Pokémon on the field wake up, and none can fall to sleep until the lock-in ends.

Pokémon cannot use REST during this effect.

This move cannot be selected by SLEEP TALK.
