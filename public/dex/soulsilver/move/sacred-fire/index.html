Moves - Sacred Fire

Categories: Ailment, Damage

The foe is razed with a mystical fire of great intensity. It may also leave the target with a burn.

Stats

TypeFire
Classphysical class
Power100
Accuracy95
PP5
TargetSelected Pokémon
PriorityNormal

Special Effects

  • Inflicts Burn on the target(s). (50% chance)

Flags

  • Blocked by Detect and Protect

    This move will not work if the target has used Detect or Protect this turn.

  • Copied by Mirror Move

    A Pokémon targeted by this move can use Mirror Move to copy it.

  • Defrosts when used

    This move can be used while frozen to force the Pokémon to defrost.

Description

Inflicts regular damage. Has a 50% chance to burn the target. Frozen Pokémon may use this move, in which case they will thaw.
