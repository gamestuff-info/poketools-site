 Origin Giratina

  • National 487

Renegade Pokémon: This Pokémon is said to live in a world on the reverse side of ours, where common knowledge is distorted and strange.

  • Ghost
  • Dragon

Forms have different stats and can learn different moves from tutors. Moves are retained through form changes. Giratina transforms into Origin Forme in the NO LINK TEXT or while holding a Griseous Orb. Otherwise, it assumes its Altered Forme.

The Griseous Orb returns to the bag upon entering the Wi-Fi Club or the Union Room.
