Moves - Role Play

Categories: Unique

The user mimics the target completely, copying the target’s natural Ability.

Special Effects

This move has no special effects.

Flags

  • Ignores Substitute

    This move ignores the target's Substitute.

Description

User's ability is replaced with the target's until the user leaves the field. Ignores accuracy and evasion modifiers.

If the target has Flower Gift, Forecast, Illusion, Imposter, Multitype, Trace, Wonder Guard, or Zen Mode, this move will fail.

This move cannot be copied by Mirror Move.
