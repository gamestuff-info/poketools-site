Moves - SPIT UP

Categories: Damage

The power built using STOCKPILE is released at once for attack.

Stats

TypeNormal
Classphysical class
Power100
Accuracy100
PP10
TargetSelected Pokémon
PriorityNormal

Special Effects

This move has no special effects.

Flags

  • Blocked by Detect and Protect

    This move will not work if the target has used DETECT or PROTECT this turn.

  • Affected by King's Rock

    Pokémon holding a KING'S ROCK have a 10% chance of causing the target to flinch.

Description

Inflicts regular damage. Power is equal to 100 times the amount of energy stored by STOCKPILE. Ignores the random factor in the damage formula. Stored energy is consumed, and the user's Defense and Special Defense are reset to what they would be if STOCKPILE had not been used. If the user has no energy stored, this move will fail.

This move cannot be copied by MIRROR MOVE.
