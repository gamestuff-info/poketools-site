Moves - Water Pledge

Categories: Damage

A column of water strikes the target. When combined with its fire equivalent, the damage increases and a rainbow appears.

Description

Inflicts regular damage. If a friendly Pokémon used Grass Pledge earlier this turn, all opposing Pokémon have halved Speed for four turns (including this one).
