Moves - Sky Attack

Categories: Damage

A second-turn attack move where critical hits land more easily. It may also make the target flinch.

Stats

TypeFlying
Classphysical class
Power140
Accuracy90
PP5
TargetSelected Pokémon
PriorityNormal

Special Effects

  • Critical hit rate increased by 1%.

Flags

  • Has a charging turn

    This move has a charging turn that can be skipped with a Power Herb.

  • Blocked by Detect and Protect

    This move will not work if the target has used Detect or Protect this turn.

  • Copied by Mirror Move

    A Pokémon targeted by this move can use Mirror Move to copy it.

  • Targets opposite side in triples

    In triple battles, this move can be used on either side to target the farthest away opposing Pokémon.

Description

Inflicts regular damage. User charges for one turn before attacking. Critical hit chance is one level higher than normal. Has a 30% chance to make the target flinch.

This move cannot be selected by Sleep Talk.
