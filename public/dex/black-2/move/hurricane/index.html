Moves - Hurricane

Categories: Ailment, Damage

The user attacks by wrapping its opponent in a fierce wind that flies up into the sky. It may also confuse the target.

Special Effects

  • Inflicts Confusion on the target(s). (30% chance)
  • Lasts 2-5 turns.

Flags

  • Blocked by Detect and Protect

    This move will not work if the target has used Detect or Protect this turn.

  • Copied by Mirror Move

    A Pokémon targeted by this move can use Mirror Move to copy it.

  • Targets opposite side in triples

    In triple battles, this move can be used on either side to target the farthest away opposing Pokémon.

Description

Inflicts regular damage. Has a 30% chance to confuse the target.

This move can hit Pokémon under the effect of Bounce, Fly, or Sky Drop.

During Rain Dance, this move has 100% accuracy. During Sunny Day, this move has 50% accuracy.
