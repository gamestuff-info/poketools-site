Moxie

Boosts Attack after knocking out any Pokémon.

Description

This Pokémon's Attack rises one stage upon knocking out another Pokémon, even a friendly Pokémon.

This ability does not take effect when the Pokémon indirectly causes another Pokémon to faint, e.g. through poison or Spikes.

If this Pokémon knocks out a Pokémon with Mummy, the former's ability will change without taking effect.
