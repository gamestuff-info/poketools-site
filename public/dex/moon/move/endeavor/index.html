Moves - Endeavor

Categories: Damage

This attack move cuts down the target’s HP to equal the user’s HP.

Stats

TypeNormal
Classphysical class
Power–
Accuracy100
PP5
TargetSelected Pokémon
PriorityNormal

Special Effects

This move has no special effects.

Flags

  • Makes contact

    User touches the target. This triggers some abilities (e.g., Static) and items (e.g., Sticky Barb).

  • Blocked by Detect and Protect

    This move will not work if the target has used Detect or Protect this turn.

  • Copied by Mirror Move

    A Pokémon targeted by this move can use Mirror Move to copy it.

Description

Inflicts exactly enough damage to lower the target's HP to equal the user's. If the target's HP is not higher than the user's, this move has no effect. Type immunity applies, but other type effects are ignored. This effect counts as damage for moves that respond to damage.
