Moves - POISON TAIL

Categories: Ailment, Damage

An attack with a high critical-hit ratio. It may also poison the foe.

Stats

TypePoison
Classphysical class
Power50
Accuracy100
PP25
TargetSelected Pokémon
PriorityNormal

Special Effects

  • Inflicts Poison on the target(s). (10% chance)
  • Critical hit rate increased by 1%.

Flags

  • Makes contact

    User touches the target. This triggers some abilities (e.g., STATIC) and items (e.g., NO LINK TEXT).

  • Blocked by Detect and Protect

    This move will not work if the target has used DETECT or PROTECT this turn.

  • Copied by Mirror Move

    A Pokémon targeted by this move can use MIRROR MOVE to copy it.

  • Affected by King's Rock

    Pokémon holding a KING'S ROCK have a 10% chance of causing the target to flinch.

Description

Inflicts regular damage. User's critical hit rate is one level higher when using this move. Has a 10% chance to poison the target.
