Moves - ENDURE

Categories: Unique

Endures any attack for 1 turn, leaving at least 1 HP.

Special Effects

This move has no special effects.

No special flags apply to this move.

Description

The user's HP cannot be lowered below 1 by any means for the remainder of this turn.

If the user successfully used DETECT, ENDURE, or PROTECT, on the last turn, this move has a 50% chance to fail.

This move cannot be selected by ASSIST or METRONOME.
