Moves - DETECT

Categories: Unique

Evades attack, but may fail if used in succession.

Special Effects

This move has no special effects.

Flags

No special flags apply to this move.

Description

No moves will hit the user for the remainder of this turn. If the user is last to act this turn, this move will fail.

If the user successfully used DETECT, ENDURE, or PROTECT on the last turn, this move has a 50% chance to fail.

LOCK-ON and MIND READER provide a100−accuracy percent chance for moves to break through this move. This does not apply to one-hit KO moves (FISSURE, GUILLOTINE, HORN DRILL, and SHEER COLD); those are always blocked by this move.

THUNDER during RAIN DANCE and BLIZZARD during HAIL have a 30% chance to break through this move.

The following effects are not prevented by this move:

  • CURSE's curse effect
  • Delayed damage from DOOM DESIRE and FUTURE SIGHT; however, these moves will be prevented if they are used this turn
  • IMPRISON
  • PERISH SONG
  • Moves that merely copy the user, such as TRANSFORM or PSYCH UP

This move cannot be selected by ASSIST or METRONOME.

Similar moves

These moves have the same effect, but their stats and effect chances may differ.
