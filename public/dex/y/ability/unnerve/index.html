Unnerve

Unnerves opposing Pokémon and makes them unable to eat Berries.

Description

Opposing Pokémon cannot eat held Berries while this Pokémon is in battle.

Affected Pokémon can still use Bug Bite or Pluck to eat a target's Berry.
