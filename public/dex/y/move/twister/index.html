Moves - Twister

Categories: Damage

The user whips up a vicious tornado to tear at the opposing Pokémon. This may also make them flinch.

Power40

PP20

Special Effects

    Flags

    • Blocked by Detect and Protect

      This move will not work if the target has used Detect or Protect this turn.

    • Copied by Mirror Move

      A Pokémon targeted by this move can use Mirror Move to copy it.

    Description

    Inflicts regular damage. Has a 20% chance to make each target flinch.

    If the target is under the effect of Bounce, Fly, or Sky Drop, this move will hit with double power.
