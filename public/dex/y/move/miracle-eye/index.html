Moves - Miracle Eye

Categories: Ailment

Enables a Dark-type target to be hit by Psychic-type attacks. This also enables an evasive target to be hit.

Flags

  • Blocked by Detect and Protect

    This move will not work if the target has used Detect or Protect this turn.

  • Reflectable

    This move may be reflected back at the user with Magic Coat or Magic Bounce.

  • Copied by Mirror Move

    A Pokémon targeted by this move can use Mirror Move to copy it.

  • Ignores Substitute

    This move ignores the target's Substitute.

Description

Resets the target's evasion to normal and prevents any further boosting until the target leaves the field. A Dark Pokémon under this effect takes normal damage from Psychic moves. This move itself ignores accuracy and evasion modifiers.
