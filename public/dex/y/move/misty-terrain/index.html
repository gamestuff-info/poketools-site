Moves - Misty Terrain

Categories: Whole Field Effect

The user covers the ground under everyone’s feet with mist for five turns. This protects Pokémon on the ground from status conditions.

For five turns, protects all Pokémon on the ground from major status ailments and confusion and weakens Dragon moves used against them to 0.5× their power.
