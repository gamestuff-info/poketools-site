Moves - Thunder Fang

Categories: Ailment, Damage

The user bites with electrified fangs. This may also make the target flinch or leave it with paralysis.

Special Effects

  • Inflicts Paralysis on the target(s). (10% chance)

Flags

  • Makes contact

    User touches the target. This triggers some abilities (e.g., Static) and items (e.g., Sticky Barb).

  • Blocked by Detect and Protect

    This move will not work if the target has used Detect or Protect this turn.

  • Copied by Mirror Move

    A Pokémon targeted by this move can use Mirror Move to copy it.

  • Jaw-based

    This move has 1.5× its usual power when used by a Pokémon with Strong Jaw.

Description

Inflicts regular damage. Has a 10% chance to paralyze the target and a separate 10% chance to make the target flinch.
