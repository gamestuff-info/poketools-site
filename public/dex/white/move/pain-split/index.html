Moves - Pain Split

Categories: Unique

The user adds its HP to the target’s HP, then equally shares the combined HP with the target.

Special Effects

Flags

  • Blocked by Detect and Protect

    This move will not work if the target has used Detect or Protect this turn.

  • Copied by Mirror Move

    A Pokémon targeted by this move can use Mirror Move to copy it.

Description

Changes the user's and target's remaining HP to the average of their current remaining HP. Ignores accuracy and evasion modifiers. This effect does not count as inflicting damage for other moves and effects that respond to damage taken.

This effect fails against a Substitute.
