Moves - False Swipe

Categories: Damage

A restrained attack that prevents the target from fainting. The target is left with at least 1 HP.

Flags

  • Blocked by Detect and Protect

    This move will not work if the target has used Detect or Protect this turn.

  • Copied by Mirror Move

    A Pokémon targeted by this move can use Mirror Move to copy it.

Description

Inflicts regular damage. Will not reduce the target's HP below 1.
