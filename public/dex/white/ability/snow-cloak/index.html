Snow Cloak

Raises evasion in a hailstorm.

Description

During hail, this Pokémon has 1.25× its evasion, and it does not take hail damage regardless of type.

The evasion bonus does not count as a stat modifier.

Overworld: If the lead Pokémon has this ability, the wild encounter rate is halved in snow.
