Moves - Grassy Terrain

Categories: Whole Field Effect

The user turns the ground under everyone’s feet to grass for five turns. This restores the HP of Pokémon on the ground a little every turn.

Description

For five turns, heals all Pokémon on the ground for 1/16 their max HP each turn and strengthens their Grass moves to 1.5× their power.
