Moves - Taunt

Categories: Unique

The target is taunted into a rage that allows it to use only attack moves for three turns.

Stats

TypeDark
Classstatus class
Power–
Accuracy100
PP20
TargetSelected Pokémon
PriorityNormal

Special Effects

This move has no special effects.

Flags

  • Blocked by Detect and Protect

    This move will not work if the target has used Detect or Protect this turn.

  • Reflectable

    This move may be reflected back at the user with Magic Coat or Magic Bounce.

  • Copied by Mirror Move

    A Pokémon targeted by this move can use Mirror Move to copy it.

  • Ignores Substitute

    This move ignores the target's Substitute.

  • Mental effects

    This move is blocked by Aroma Veil and cured by Mental Herb.

Description

Target is forced to only use damaging moves for the next 3–5 turns, selected at random. Moves that select other moves not known in advance do not count as damaging.

Assist, Copycat, Me First, Metronome, Mirror Move, and Sleep Talk do not directly inflict damage and thus may not be used.

Bide, Counter, Endeavor, Metal Burst, and Mirror Coat are allowed.

Similar moves

These moves have the same effect, but their stats and effect chances may differ.
