Moves - Parting Shot

Categories: Net Good Stats

With a parting threat, the user lowers the target’s Attack and Sp. Atk stats. Then it switches with a party Pokémon.

Stats

TypeDark
Classstatus class
Power–
Accuracy100
PP20
TargetSelected Pokémon
PriorityNormal

Special Effects

  • Attack decreased by 1 (100% chance)
  • Special Attack decreased by 1 (100% chance)

Flags

  • Blocked by Detect and Protect

    This move will not work if the target has used Detect or Protect this turn.

  • Reflectable

    This move may be reflected back at the user with Magic Coat or Magic Bounce.

  • Copied by Mirror Move

    A Pokémon targeted by this move can use Mirror Move to copy it.

  • Sound-based

    Pokémon with Soundproof are immune to this move.

  • Ignores Substitute

    This move ignores the target's Substitute.

Description

Lowers all targets' Attack and Special Attack by one stage. Makes the user switch out.
